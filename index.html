About Me

I graduated cum laude in 2024 with a bachelor's degree in Game Development (specialization in Game Sound Integration) from Digital Arts & Entertainment.

From 2019 to 2021, I studied Independent Game Development because, in high school, I was fascinated by how video games combined art and programming to allow people to experience beautiful stories. During a group project in my 4th semester, I discovered a love for audio, after which I decided to pivot to Game Sound Integration (2021–2024).

My main specialization is programming. C# is my strongest language, which I have been working with since high school. I also have experience working in C++. Recently, I've developed a strong interest in Python because of its usage in AI. Although I’m specialized in programming, I also enjoy creating visual and auditory art and am familiar with the software used to create these, such as Reaper, Blender, and Photoshop.

I'm passionate about exploring and learning new technologies and finding ways to use them in meaningful ways. I enjoy being challenged and exploring outside of my comfort zone. The two technologies I've been mainly exploring are Extended Reality (XR) and Artificial Intelligence (AI). Two of the latest projects featured in my portfolio highlight my work with XR. As for AI, I’m actively learning and exploring its possibilities.
