About Me

About Me I graduated cum laude in 2024 with a Bachelor’s degree in Game Development (specializing in Game Sound Integration) from Digital Arts & Entertainment at Howest. From 2019 to 2021, I studied Independent Game Development (2021-2024) because I was drawn to the idea of creating and managing my own projects, showcasing my entrepreneurial mindset. During a group project in my fourth semester, I discovered my passion for sound design. That summer, I decided to pivot and change my major to Game Sound Integration, aligning my studies with my newfound interest.

My main specialization is programming, with extensive experience in C#, a language I began learning in high school. I also have experience working with C++, and recently, I’ve developed a keen interest in Python due to its applications in AI. I enjoy tackling challenges and creating meaningful projects by leveraging new and emerging technologies.

I’m incredibly grateful for the opportunity to start my professional journey at DAE Research. It was here that I delved into cutting-edge technologies like XR and AI, learning not just about the tools, but also the value of teamwork and collaboration. The inspiring people I’ve worked with have shaped my approach to problem-solving and innovation.

Two of the latest projects featured in my portfolio highlight my work with XR. As for AI, I’m still exploring its possibilities, which is why I don’t yet have AI-based projects to showcase. For the future, I aim to build solutions that not only push technological boundaries but also positively impact the people who interact with them. To me, innovation is not just about technology—it’s about connecting with people and making a difference.
