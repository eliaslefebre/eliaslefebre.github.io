Types of Toys:

1. Hands-Free Toys

The first type of toys are toys that don't require you to move your hands at all. In the video to the right, every time the guitar gets plucked, a sample is played according to where the user looks.

2. Hybrid Toys

The second type of toys combine a virtual object with a real-world instrument. You have to interact with both to get the desired sound. In the video to the right, depending on where balls are in orbit, the piano played will sound different.

3. Purely Virtual Toys

The third type of toys are purely virtual. These are virtual objects. In the video on the right, you can play a pitch depending on where you hold your hand.

There were plenty of more toys created during this project.
