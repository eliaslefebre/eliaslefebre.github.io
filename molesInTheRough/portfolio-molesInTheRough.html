Summary:

Join the exciting adventure of Moles in the Rough! Operate a mining drill with your team, digging deep into the planet's core to collect valuable gems. Your goal is to gather as many gems as possible while managing resources, completing tasks, and making strategic decisions. Be wary of the boss, who may cut costs by firing miners with fewer gems. Your skills and strategy will ensure your job security and safe return to the surface in this thrilling mining adventure!
