Summary

A co-op game made during the Unwrap Game Jam of 2023.

You play as one of two young wizards-in-training tasked with making potions for their demanding supervisor. Using delicate and colorful materials in shades of green, blue, and red, you must carefully combine ingredients to create the perfect concoction. These potions, in turn, can also be combined in new and interesting combinations.

But be careful! One wrong move and the potion could fail, leaving you with nothing to sell and a disappointed boss. Can you master the art of potion-making and prove yourself to be a true wizard? Only time will tell in this magical and challenging game.
