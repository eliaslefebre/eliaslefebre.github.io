3 Museos Game Project

Description: The request for this game came from 3 Museos, and thanks to DAE's applied games program, we had the chance to collaborate on this endeavor. Over a span of approximately four months, a team of six individuals, including myself, worked on this project. The primary objective was to create an enjoyable and educational game that visitors to a museum in Mexico could enjoy.

My Contribution:

  • Programming in C#
  • SFX creation
  • Foley recording and editing
  • Music composition
  • Audio implementation in Unity

Challenges:

This game inspired me to delve deeper into Sound Design. It was the first project I designed sounds for, and I found it easy to get into a flow when working on audio. Thanks to this project, I decided to switch majors from Independent Game Development to Sound Design after this year.

It was my first opportunity to become familiar with the process of sound design, sound implementation, and music implementation. I built a strong foundation during this project, which I further honed in the years to come.
